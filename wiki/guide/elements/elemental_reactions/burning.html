Burning ​

    < Elemental Reactions

Burning is the Elemental Reaction triggered when Pyro is applied onto an entity already affected by Dendro or vice versa.

This reaction deals AoE Pyro DMG and applies 1 gauge unit of Pyro to all entities in a 1m radius. This Pyro application has an Internal Cooldown of 2 seconds.

Internal Data ​

Reaction Multiplier: 0.25
Reaction ID(s):

  • seven-elements:burning
  • seven-elements:burning_quicken (triggered on the Quicken aura)
